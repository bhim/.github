BHIM (Bharat Interface for Money) @ NPCI

Following are some of the facts about the solution we operate:

Instant Money Transfer: Real-time money transfer app built on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI).

24x7 Availability: Operates round the clock for seamless transactions.

Interoperability: Send and request money using just a Mobile Number, UPI ID, or QR code.

Wide Accessibility: Available in multiple preferred languages for diverse user bases.

Security: Designed for simple and secure transactions on the go.

Features Stack at BHIM

Unified Transaction Interface: Powers instant money transfers to friends, relatives, online shopping sites, retailers, or merchants.

UPI AUTOPAY: Framework to set recurring payments on BHIM UPI enabled apps.

IPO on UPI: Facilitates subscriptions to Initial Public Offerings using the UPI framework.

OD/CC on UPI: Support for Overdraft and Credit Card linkage on UPI.

*Offline Payments (99#): Enables transactions without internet connectivity.

Bill Payments: Integrated platform for paying utility bills and other expenses.

Get Started

BHIM powers India's digital payment ecosystem by allowing users to make cashless payments directly through their mobile phones.

Who can use BHIM?

Anyone with a valid Bank Account.

A Registered Mobile Number linked to the account.

A valid Debit Card.

How to join:

Download the BHIM App from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

Verify your mobile number.

Link your bank account and set your UPI PIN.

About BHIM

BHIM (Bharat Interface for Money) is an app that lets you make simple, easy, and quick transactions using Unified Payments Interface (UPI). It is a direct initiative to enable fast, secure, reliable cashless payments through your mobile phone.
